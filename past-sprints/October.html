    Chestist

    Helper app for radiologist to flag patients with different anomalies in the lungs.

    This will help the healthcare worker to speed up the workload in case of having a huge influx of patients coming and shortage of staff and/or to help out the radiologist to have a second look at the same radiography.

    Powered by deep learning to achieve the image anomaly detection portion with 60% plus accuracy.

    Leveraging Densenet121 trained with imagenet, kubernetes, pytorch. And Microsoft's Blazor for frontend.
