    FhirBlaze - FHIR/GraphQL/Blazor

    What do you get when you combine FHIR, GraphQL, and Blazor?

    FHIRBlaze!

    There have been a lot of talks around leveraging FHIR as the SOR of healthcare data. However, there are not many tutorials out there on how you can leverage FHIR with trending framework like GraphQL and Blazor.

    We've done the research for you and created a starter-kit you can simply download and run locally!

    In our repo you will find our "Gotchas!" and "Ah hah!" moment we experienced along the way.

    Check it out!
