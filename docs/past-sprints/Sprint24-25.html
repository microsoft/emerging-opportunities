    TechSerum Hackathon (6/11 - 6/13)

    Microsoft sponsored hackathon (with Whyse)

    Challenge: Improve patients’ access to better healthcare.

    This hackathon married talented community developers and highly influencial individuals from the medical field to tackle the given challenge.

    Bringing medical expertise/experiences and developers allowed them to quickly create apps that deal with matching clinical trial datas with patient records. Some solutions involved creating their own social network!

    During our three day hackathon our goal as a team was to help the participating teams ideate, design, and build their first mvps!

    Verifiable Credentials

    Based on Microsoft's Azure AD Verifiable Credential service (Public Preview.)

    We've worked with the VC team on a simple usecase where an employees receives employment credentials by logging into their companies internal portal to showcase the features and capabilities of Verifiable Credentials (VC)

    We've leveraged these concepts listed below:

    Verifiable Credentials (VC)

    Genomics Hackathon

    To help our customers derive insights and information from the growing volume of sequencing data our team put together a one-day hackathon focused on Genomics.

    Our goal was to get more people comfortable with what our customers are working on and the tools that we have for them to accomplish their goals.

    Why Genomics on Azure?

    Drug Discovery
    As we've seen with COVID-19 vaccines, understanding the viral genome has led to vaccines produced at remarkable pace.

    Precision medicine
    By understanding both the disease as well as the patient's genome, pharma companies can produce more targeted therapies.

    Rare diseases
    Some of the most devasting diseases can now be better understood using genetics which will lead to cures.
